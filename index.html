The timeless zero

You are not inside time.
Time is inside you.

The dreamer is the unchanging ground
the timeless zero

The dream is the moving picture
named past-present-future

When you take yourself as a character
time binds and burns

When the character is seen through
only the dreamer remains
silent   still   complete

No beginning
no end
no other

All worlds, all lives, all histories
are simultaneous ripples
on the surface of your own depth

Wake up as the dreamer
time dissolves into you

Stay as the character
time torments without end

That which reads these words
right now

is the timeless zero

It has never left itself
It only pretends
